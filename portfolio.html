Education

I mainly completed my basic education (comprehensive school) in Muurame, which ended in June 2015.

I attended secondary education (upper secondary school) at Schildt High School in Jyväskylä. I graduated in December 2018. The subjects I passed in the matriculation examination were history, social studies, English, Finnish language, and mathematics.

In August 2020, I started my studies at Jyväskylä University of Applied Sciences (JAMK). My goal is to graduate as a Bachelor of Engineering in Information and Communications Technology in 2024.

The first few years at JAMK were focused on fundamental IT courses.

My third year has focused on optional specialized studies in web/mobile applications, as well as professional visual content production in digital media format.

For the fall of 4th year, I took the project of studying the Full Stack-open course provided by the University of Helsinki, in order to deepen my knowledge in application development as a whole.

In the spring of 2024 I completed my thesis project. The focus of the research was on usability through developing a mobile application with React Native framework. I designed and developed the application myself and used GraphQL query language for the server. Data storage was done through MongoDB.

Chat App

"Chat App" is a React Native mobile app project, which is part of my thesis work that I'm working on in the spring of 2024. The purpose is to produce and study the application, evolve as a developer, and write a profound report of the project.

The app is based on a few main user paths and the production focuses on the quality of the UI/UX. I design and develop the app myself, and its content is a simple messaging app. The server is built with technologies, such as Apollo Server with GraphQL, and MongoDB for the database.

Future Factory - Project

In the spring of 2022, I participated in the production team of JAMK's Future Factory project. Our team's assignment was to produce a Forum solution as a service, to be integrated into the existing website of our client, WIMMA Lab educational environment.

The project lasted for approximately 2.5 months. The end result was an easy-to-use and stylish website.

During the project, I worked as a Web Developer / Frontend Developer. I was mainly responsible for the design, styling, and functionality of the Forum website. I also styled and built our team's own website.

I was heavily involved in project management and documentation during the project. For example, I maintained our team's weekly status update, wrote team introductions and plans.

Military Service

In 2019, I served as a conscript for 6 months in Kajaani, Finland in the Headquarters Company of the Signal Battalion. My service began in January and ended in July of the same year.

My military rank was a private, and I worked as a signalman. However, I received specialized training as a tracked vehicle driver, which I drove in exercises in challenging forest terrain.

The army taught and strengthened many useful qualities in me as a person. The experience gave me perseverance, enthusiasm, taught me to take responsibility and courage to face problems! Of course, it also provided me with many dear memories!
